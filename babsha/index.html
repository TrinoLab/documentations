Overview

Let me show you some features of BABSHA .

You can add edit and delete products form Products menu 

 

 

You can see your customer form customer menue . NB: Here customers are who sent message to your page .

 

 

 

 

When your customers will place orders via Facebook messenger you will find them at Orders menue 

After receiving payment click confirm button then a receipt will send to your customer . You can also delete orders . If your customers have no shipping address in address fields then you can request your customer to update their information by clicking "Request for update address" button then a message will send to your customer to update their information and you can also send a message by clicking "Send message button"

 

After confirming orders you can see your earning on the top of the menu bar 

You can also change your money unit form settings . 

  You can send mass message to your customers by clicking Mass Message menue
